Testing can refer to various activities depending on the context. Here are a few common meanings:

Software Testing: This involves the process of evaluating a software application to identify any bugs, errors, or defects. It ensures that the software functions correctly and meets the specified requirements.

Medical Testing: In the context of healthcare, testing can refer to diagnostic procedures or examinations to determine the presence or absence of a particular condition or disease.

Product Testing: This involves evaluating a product's performance, quality, or safety  various tests before it is released to the market.
